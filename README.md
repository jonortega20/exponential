## How I Work

**Gets me pumped:**
Working with people that deliver. Anything or anyone that challenges me. Shipping fast and iterating. New things, I'm always tinkering.

**Disappoints me:**
Bureaucracy with no purpose. Not finding the reason for something. Marginal improvements disguised as ambition. Working on things I don't believe in, I can't fake it. Big egos.

**Strengths:**
People that know me say the main one is pace of learning (taught myself lots of things). I'd say adaptability to different environments, discipline (motivation is not long-term) and pragmatism. I also like the concepts of agency and taste. I try to over-communicate and think long-term.

**Improvement points:**
I sometimes assume things instead of asking. I'm working on being more explicit (prompting Claude Code has actually helped me with this). I can be impatient with slow-moving processes.

**Comms style:**
Inbox-zero. Async-first. Fan of Loom for async walkthroughs. I avoid Calendly — friction in scheduling helps me say no to unnecessary meetings. When giving feedback, I focus on the big picture, not the details.
